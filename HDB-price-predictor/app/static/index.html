About this HDB Resale Price Estimator

This free tool estimates the resale price of any HDB flat in Singapore using a machine learning model trained on 227,000+ transactions from 2017 to present. It covers all 26 HDB towns and flat types — from 1 Room to Multi-Generation — and returns an instant price estimate based on location, storey, floor area, lease remaining, and proximity to amenities such as MRT stations, schools, hawker centres, and shopping malls.

The model achieves R² = 0.980 with a typical prediction error of around S$33,000, making it one of the most accurate publicly available HDB resale price calculators in Singapore.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this HDB resale price estimate?
The model has an R² score of 0.980 — meaning it explains 98% of price variation across all HDB flats. The typical prediction error is around S$33,000. Results are most accurate for standard flat types in established estates with good transaction history.
What data is this HDB price calculator based on?
The model is trained on 227,425 HDB resale transactions from January 2017 to present, sourced from data.gov.sg. Features include distance to CBD, MRT stations, elite primary schools, hawker centres, shopping malls, and parks — all geocoded using the OneMap API.
Which HDB towns and flat types are supported?
All 26 HDB towns in Singapore are supported, including Ang Mo Kio, Bedok, Bishan, Bukit Merah, Clementi, Hougang, Jurong East, Kallang/Whampoa, Punggol, Queenstown, Sengkang, Tampines, Toa Payoh, Woodlands, and Yishun. Flat types from 1 Room through Executive and Multi-Generation are all covered.
Can I use this as an official HDB valuation?
No. This tool provides an indicative estimate based on historical transaction data and machine learning. It is not an official valuation. For formal valuations required for bank loans or CPF usage, you must engage a licensed property valuer or use HDB's official e-Valuation service.
